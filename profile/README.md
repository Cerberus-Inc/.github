<h1 align="center">CERBERUS INC</h1>

---

Cerberus Inc is a high-security distributed systems company building next-generation corporate data-protection infrastructure.  
Our platform combines a resilient blockchain foundation with a multi-sharded network architecture designed to remain operational and secure under any conditions.  
The core philosophy of Cerberus is simple: **data must remain protected, available, and tamper-proof no matter where it lives or how it moves.**

We deliver a fully isolated corporate environment built around a private Tendermint-based consensus engine, enhanced with a custom multi-shard communication layer and intelligent routing mechanisms.  
Our security stack integrates advanced AI-driven analysis, anomaly detection, and a tailored Moving Target Defense strategy—continuously reshaping the attack surface and dramatically reducing exploitation windows.  
All communication between nodes is authenticated, encrypted, and passed through adaptive pathways that respond in real time to network behavior, threat levels, and system load.

Beyond the core distributed ledger, Cerberus includes an internal corporate messenger with reinforced cryptographic guarantees and multi-layer protection.  
This communication system is engineered for organizations that cannot afford data leaks, metadata exposure, or uncontrolled internal access. It operates as a secure enclave within the broader architecture, maintaining high throughput without sacrificing confidentiality or integrity.

Cerberus is designed to be deployed inside highly regulated environments, air-gapped infrastructures, or large organizations that require an uncompromising level of control over their information ecosystem.  
Every component of the platform—from consensus and replication to identity and transport—has been built to minimize trust assumptions and maximize operational resilience.
